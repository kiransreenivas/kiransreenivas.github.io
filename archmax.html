The Problem

Flat feet (pes planus) affects approximately 20% of all humans — a condition where the medial arch of the foot collapses, causing over-pronation. In football, this creates serious biomechanical consequences: altered running mechanics, reduced sprint efficiency, ankle instability, and a significantly higher risk of injury.

Research confirms that flat-footed athletes have a 50% higher injury risk than those with normal arches, and are more susceptible to conditions including plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy, shin splints, patellofemoral pain syndrome, and ankle sprains — all injuries that can end or shorten a football career.

Despite this clear need, the existing market offers no affordable, personalised, integrated solution. Competitors either sell expensive standalone insoles without customisation, or have exited the football market entirely.

The Solution
3D-Printed Personalised Football Boots — "Fit It Your Way"
Arch MAX — "Fit It Your Way" — the brand built around personalised biomechanical footwear
1
Scan Your Foot
Customer uses the Arch MAX app to generate a 3D model of their foot using their smartphone camera. The app determines exact foot type, arch height, and dimensional requirements.
2
3D Print in ~1 Hour
Dimensions are sent directly to a Formlabs Fuse 1+ 30W SLS 3D printer, which produces a personalised pair of insoles in approximately one hour using TPU powder.
3
Assemble & Ship
Insoles are foam-coated for comfort and inserted into pre-manufactured wide toe-box football shoe shells sourced from a verified Chinese manufacturer. Delivered in 2–3 days.

Competitive advantage: VKTRY insoles cost €200+ per pair. Stride Soles Custom Soccer Orthotics cost €149+ per pair. Neither is integrated with a football shoe. Arch MAX delivers a fully personalised, integrated boot at €79.99 — making it the only affordable, custom, end-to-end solution in the European market.
